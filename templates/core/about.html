About Freeparty

Freeparty is a social platform built around conversation, self-expression, and community. The goal is to give people a place to post, connect, and build a presence online without turning the experience into a cluttered, hostile, or manipulative feed.

What Freeparty is for

Freeparty is designed to work as both a social feed and a personal identity layer. You can use it to post updates, follow people, share media and links, message others, and maintain a profile that acts like a lightweight home on the web.

What we care about

  • Clear identity and profile tools
  • Simple posting and discovery
  • Safer defaults and moderation support
  • Useful features without unnecessary noise
  • A foundation that can grow over time

How the platform is built

Freeparty is being developed as a centralized-first platform with room to grow into federation later. That means it is focused on working well as a single deployable service today, while keeping space in the architecture for future interoperability.

Our approach

We want Freeparty to feel expressive, human, and manageable. That means balancing open participation with moderation tools, abuse prevention, and room for communities to feel safe.

Freeparty is still evolving. Features, design, and policies may change as the platform grows, but the core idea stays the same: give people useful social tools with better control over their experience.

Support

Need help with Freeparty? This page is the best place to start.

Account help

If you are having trouble signing in, verifying your email, or accessing your account, include:

  • Your username
  • The email address tied to the account
  • A short description of the problem
  • The approximate date and time the issue happened

Safety and abuse issues

For harassment, impersonation, threats, spam, or other harmful behavior, use in-app report and block tools when available. Include links, usernames, timestamps, and any relevant context.

Bug reports

When reporting a bug, include:

  • What you were trying to do
  • What happened instead
  • What device/browser you were using
  • Any error message you saw
  • Steps to reproduce the issue, if known

Response expectations

Support priority may vary depending on the type of issue. Safety, access, and platform-stability issues are usually treated as the highest priority.

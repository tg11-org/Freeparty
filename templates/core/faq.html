Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to post to use Freeparty?

No. You can use your profile and links page as a standalone presence.

Can I make my account private?

Yes. Enable private account in profile settings.

How do I report abuse?

Use report and block actions on profiles and content.

How do link previews work?

Link previews are fetched once and stored, so pages are not refetched on every view.

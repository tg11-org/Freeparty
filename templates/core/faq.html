Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to post to use Freeparty?

No. You can keep a profile, follow people, browse public content, and use your account as a lightweight online presence without posting regularly.

Can I make my account private?

If private-account controls are available in your settings, yes. Private or limited visibility features may continue to evolve as Freeparty grows.

How do I report abuse or harmful content?

Use the in-app reporting and safety tools when available. Include as much context as possible so moderators can review the issue quickly.

How do blocking and muting work?

Blocking is for stronger separation and safety. Muting is for reducing unwanted visibility without fully cutting off access. Exact behavior may depend on the feature set currently live.

Can I delete my posts?

In general, yes, though some copies, logs, moderation references, or backups may remain for a limited period where needed for safety, legal, or operational reasons.

How do link previews work?

Link previews may be fetched and stored so posts can display rich previews without refetching the page every time someone views it.

Will Freeparty support federation?

The platform is being built with federation-readiness in mind, but the primary focus right now is a solid centralized experience first.

Where do I get help?

Start with the Support page. For safety issues, use reporting tools where available.

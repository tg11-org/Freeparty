Freeparty collects and processes information needed to operate the platform, protect accounts, enforce policies, and improve reliability.

1. Information we collect

  • Account information, such as email address, username, and password hash

  • To authenticate users and secure accounts

3. How information is shared

Public profile information and content you choose to publish may be visible to other users and visitors. We may also disclose information when required by law, to protect users, or to maintain the security and integrity of the platform.

4. Data retention

We retain information for as long as needed to operate the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, investigate abuse, and enforce our policies. Some records, logs, or moderation data may be retained after content is deleted when necessary for safety or security reasons.

5. Your choices

  • You can edit certain account and profile information
  • You can delete content you have posted, subject to system and legal retention needs
  • You can use available privacy and safety controls such as block and mute tools

6. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect user information, but no service can guarantee absolute security. You are also responsible for keeping your credentials safe.

7. Children

Freeparty is not intended for users below the minimum age required by applicable law or by the platform's own policy.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of Freeparty after the effective date of an updated policy means the revised policy applies going forward.

9. Contact

For privacy questions or requests, use the Support page.

Privacy Policy

{% include "partials/policy_meta.html" %}

Freeparty collects and processes information needed to operate the platform, protect accounts, enforce policies, and improve reliability.

1. Information we collect

  • Account information, such as email address, username, and password hash
  • Profile information you choose to provide
  • Posts, replies, messages, reports, and other content you submit
  • Social activity such as follows, likes, reposts, blocks, mutes, and bookmarks
  • Technical and operational data such as IP address, device/browser metadata, and logs

2. How we use information

  • To provide and maintain core platform features
  • To authenticate users and secure accounts
  • To detect abuse, spam, fraud, and policy violations
  • To respond to support requests and moderation reports
  • To monitor performance, troubleshoot failures, and improve the service

3. Cookies and session data

Freeparty uses essential cookies/session tokens for sign-in, security, and feature continuity. We may also store local browser preferences (for example, theme or accessibility settings). For end-to-end private messages, device-local key material may also be stored by the browser. If cookies/local storage are cleared and the active DM/PM keys for that device are removed, encrypted message content on that device may become unrecoverable.

4. How information is shared

Public profile information and content you choose to publish may be visible to other users and visitors. We may also disclose information when required by law, to protect users, or to maintain the security and integrity of the platform.

5. Data retention

  • Core account and profile data: retained while account remains active.
  • Operational logs and abuse-prevention records: retained for a limited security window, then rotated or deleted.
  • Moderation and enforcement records: may be retained longer where needed for safety, legal obligations, or repeat-abuse prevention.
  • Backup copies: retained on a rolling schedule for disaster recovery.

6. Your controls and requests

  • You can edit account and profile information in-app.
  • You can delete content you posted, subject to legal/security retention constraints.
  • You can request account deletion and privacy review via Support.
  • You can request a copy of available account data via Support.

7. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect user information, but no service can guarantee absolute security. You are also responsible for keeping your credentials safe.

8. Children and minors

Freeparty is not intended for users below the minimum age required by applicable law in their jurisdiction. Additional controls and restrictions may apply to minors where required. Where available, minor-account parental controls can require guardian email verification and guardian approval before protected privacy/content settings are changed.

9. Cross-border processing

Depending on where infrastructure is hosted, data may be processed in jurisdictions different from your own, subject to applicable legal safeguards.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of Freeparty after the effective date of an updated policy means the revised policy applies going forward.

11. Account deactivation and deletion retention

  • Account deactivation: retained for up to 12 months to support account recovery.
  • Account deletion request: scheduled with a 30-day retention window before final deletion.
  • During each retention period, recovery or cancellation links may restore account access.

12. Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact support@tg11.org.

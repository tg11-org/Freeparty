Community Guidelines

Help keep Freeparty useful, creative, and welcoming.

Be respectful

Disagree with ideas, not identity. Harassment, threats, and coordinated abuse are not allowed.

No spam or manipulation

Do not run scams, engagement bait farms, or deceptive promotion schemes.

Use content labels honestly

Mark sensitive content appropriately and avoid posting harmful material.

Report issues

If you see harmful behavior, use report and block tools so moderators can respond quickly.
